Winner of the Conrad Hilton Award for the highest scores in customer satisfaction and quality in the Hampton hotel family and a five-time winner of the Hampton Circle of Excellence Award, the Hampton Inn & Suites Green Hills offers truly superior lodging and amenities.

This signature property features traditional architecture with limestone accents. The aura of stately tradition is echoed in the interior of our boutique hotel, with cherry and mahogany woods, marble, and finely appointed public areas.

Each spacious guestroom and luxurious suite features marble showers and granite vanities, lighted makeup mirrors, and classic furnishings. A complimentary deluxe breakfast is offered to all registered guests.

For your convenience, free internet access and free local phone calls are provided in all our rooms and suites. We also provide 25" color TVs with HBO and a menu of on-demand movies. In each room and suite you will find thoughtful conveniences: a coffee maker, hair dryer, iron, and ironing board. Complimentary tea and coffee are served in the lobby 24 hours a day. We offer an exercise room that overlooks the pool and heated whirlpool, valet laundry service and guest laundry facilities, and a Suite Shop stocked with necessities and convenience items. A complimentary USA Today newspaper will be delivered to your door each weekday.

The Hampton Inn & Suites of Green Hills is also conveniently located within walking distance of many of Green Hills' neighborhood attractions. Spend time at the Green Hills Mall, catch the latest movie at the Regal Green Hills 16 Cinema, or simply stop and grab a bite to eat at any one of the numerous dining options all conveniently located right around the corner!
